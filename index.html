I put together this site to act as a portfolio of some of my design work that I hope those of you at the MMRL will find interesting.

In 2017 Westfield Shopping Malls renovated their flagship mall in Paramus New Jersey. The architect specified terrariums for the food court. I designed them. A shop in Massachusets did the metal fabrication. A shop in Maine fabricated the glass and did the final installation.
